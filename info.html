Abilities

Gadget

Hosomaki Healing

This Gadget lets Kenji instantly heal 50% of the damage he took over the last 3 seconds. It's especially useful after taking significant damage, helping him recover and stay in the fight longer.

Star Power

Nigiri Nemesis

After Kenji avoids taking damage for 5 seconds, he gains a shield that reduces the next incoming damage by 60%. This defensive boost helps him survive burst attacks and makes him more durable in close combat situations.

Hypercharge

All You Can Eat

When activated, it enhances his Super ability, Slashimi, by pulling enemies toward the center of his slashing attack. This makes it easier to hit multiple opponents and deal more damage. Additionally, while Hypercharged, Kenji gains increased movement speed and damage output, making him more powerful in battles.
